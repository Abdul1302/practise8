Typesetters have traditionally used an em as the standard
size for a paragraph indent. What’s an em? Back in the days
of hot metal typesetting, an em was literally the width of 
the letter “m” in a given typeface and type size. With 
digital type, there’s no need to measure the letter “m.”

your type. If the text is set in 12-point type, use a 12
